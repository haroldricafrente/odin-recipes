Odin Recipes: Mango Graham Cake

Description

Christmas is near and one of the famous dessert that you can see in your aunts table is the Mango Graham Cake

Not just because it's Delicous, its also relatively easy to make and budget friendly. All you just need are milk, mango, and graham crackers

How to make a Mango Graham Cake
Prep Time: 10 Minutes
Set Time: at least 4 hours but best overnight

Ingredients

  • 2 Ripe Mangoes
  • 1 Pint / 475 mls of Whipping Cream
  • 14oz / 400g of Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1 Packet of Graham Crackers

Instructions

  1. Peel, slice and dice the mango into small cubes. Pour the cream into a bowl and whip until you have stiff peaks. Add the condensed milk and stir through until mixed. Place a layer of the the Graham Crackers into your dish. Spoon on some of the cream and spread over the crackers. Cover the cream with a layer of the dices mangoes.
  2. Repeat steps 4 to 6 once. Top with the last layer of crackers. Cover the crackers with the cream and make as smooth as you can. Crush up some of the crackers till you have fine cracker crumbs and sprinkle on top of the cream. Place in the fridge to chill right through and allow the crackers to soften up and become fluffy.
  3. Once the Graham Cake has chilled, it is ready to be served. You can either spoon it into dishes or slice it and gently lift the slice on to a plate. Dig in and enjoy!
