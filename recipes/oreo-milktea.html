Odin Recipes: Oreo Cheeseckae Milktea

Description

This my favorite beverage/dessert. Yes it might be bad because of sugar but It always fill my heart with joy

The ingredient is relatively easy to find and make.

How to make Oreo Cheesecake Milktea
Prep Time: 15 Minutes
Set Time: 3 Minutes

Ingredients

  • Oreo
  • Milk
  • Ice
  • Tea
  • Boba

Instructions

  1. Make tea. Let steep, remove bag & let cool down completely.
  2. Crush Oreos either in a ziplock bag, blender, or on a chopping board
  3. Add tapioca pearls to a pan full of boiling hot water. Let simmer for 5-6 mins. Reserve 3 tablespoon of boiling water then remove pearls from heat and strain through a sieve. Add the reserved water & sugar. Stir until combined.
  4. To serve, add tapioca balls to a large glass. Serve with crushed ice if desired.
  5. Pour cool tea on top.
  6. Add crushed Oreos to milk. Stir well and pour the mixture on top of your tea.
