Odin Recipes: Filipino-Style Fried Chicken

Description

This is the classic filipino food. Wherever you are, you will alwys see fried chicken in the menu.

This specific recipe highlights the classic Filipino-style Fried Chicken that every kids and adults love

How to make Filipino-style Fried Chicken
Prep Time: 10 Minutes
Marinate Time: 30 Minutes preferebly overnight

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Egg
  • Fish sauce
  • Cornstarch
  • Flour
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Calamnsi

Instructions

  1. Beat the egg and the fish sauce together lightly in a bowl.
  2. Marinate the chicken with the egg mixture for 30 minutes to 2 hours.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, cornstarch, salt, ground black pepper, garlic powder, and baking powder.
  4. Heat oil for frying.
  5. Dip the marinated chicken one at a time into the flour mixture. Shake the chicken slightly to remove excess flour.
  6. Place the chicken in the heated oil and fry the chicken until golden brown.
  7. Serve and enjoy with your favorite dipping sauce.
